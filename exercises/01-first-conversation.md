# Exercise 1 — Your First Conversation

**Time:** 3 minutes
**Goal:** Get comfortable with how Claude reads your workspace.

This is optional. Skip if you're already chatting comfortably.

---

## Try this

Type or say something like:

> Show me what files are in this folder and explain in plain language what each one is for.

Claude should:
- List the files (CLAUDE.md, state.md, inbox/scratch.md, context/, etc.)
- Explain each one in 1-2 sentences

If something doesn't make sense, ask:

> What does CLAUDE.md actually do?
>
> Why is there both an `inbox` and a `context` folder?
>
> What happens if I delete `state.md`?

There are no wrong questions here. The goal is to make the structure feel like *your* structure, not some opaque thing somebody set up for you.

## What you'll notice

- Claude already knows things about you from the onboarding — your name, your focus, your skill level
- It's read CLAUDE.md and state.md before responding
- Files you didn't write yourself (like the skill files in `.claude/skills/`) it can also explain — though most of the time you don't need to think about those

## When to come back to this

If after a few weeks the structure feels confusing again, redo this exercise. It's a good way to recalibrate.
